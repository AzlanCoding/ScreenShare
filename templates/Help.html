Help

Having connection issues? Try the following:

Check if your device is supported

SceenShare is currently only supported on Desktop versions of Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge and Firefox on Windows, Ubuntu and ChromeOS. ChromeOS users may have trouble recieving screen sharing.

Turn off VPN/Proxy

ScreenShare uses WebRTC which is a peer-to-peer connection, meaning that ScreenShare may not work with a VPN or proxy to connect to displays.

Check your internet firewall

ScreenShare uses WebRTC which may attempt to use UDP or TCP to connect to displays. If your internet firewall blocks UDP or TCP, ScreenShare may not work. To work around this, create a Wifi Hotspot on either the sharing computer or the receiving computer. Both computers' firewalls must allow UDP and TCP traffic.
